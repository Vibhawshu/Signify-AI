Legal Rights & Schemes

RPWD Act 2016 IMPORTANT

The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act guarantees fundamental rights and accessibility:

  • 5% reservation in government jobs and higher education
  • Free education for children up to age 18
  • Mandatory accessibility standards for public spaces
  • Legal protection against discrimination
